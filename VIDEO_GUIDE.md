# Auto Diary Website Video Guide

Ye guide screen recording video banane ke liye hai. Aap mobile ya laptop screen record karke isi script ke hisab se website explain kar sakte hain.

## Video Title

Auto Diary Website Kaise Use Karein - Daily Earning, Expense aur Bank Deposit

## Video Length

Target length: 3 se 5 minutes

## Recording Se Pehle

1. Website open rakhein.
2. Google Sheet open rakhein.
3. Test user account create karne ke liye username, PAN, DOB aur password ready rakhein.
4. Ek sample entry ke liye values ready rakhein:
   - Cash Earning: `800`
   - Online Earning: `350`
   - Expense: `200`
   - Expense Reason: `CNG`

## Scene 1: Intro

Screen: Website login page

Bolna:

Namaste, ye Auto Diary website hai. Is website ki madad se auto driver apni daily earning, expense, saving aur bank deposit ka pura hisab Google Sheet me save kar sakta hai. Isme login system, daily one entry rule, earnings report aur reminder APK option bhi hai.

## Scene 2: Login

Screen: Login page

Action:

1. Pehle `Create New Account` click karein.
2. Name, username, PAN number, date of birth aur password fill karke account create karein.
3. Login tab par wapas jaakar `Login as User` select karein.
4. Username aur password se login karein.

Bolna:

Ab har driver ka apna account hoga. Register karte time name, username, PAN number, date of birth aur password save hota hai. Password Google Sheet me plain text me nahi dikhta, hash form me save hota hai. Login ke baad driver ko apna dashboard dikhega.

## Scene 3: Dashboard Aur Wallet Card

Screen: Dashboard top section aur Money Wallet card

Bolna:

Yahan Money Wallet card me current available amount dikhta hai. Cash available alag dikhega, online available alag dikhega, aur total deposited amount bhi dikhega. Available amount earning me se expense minus karke calculate hota hai. Agar main deposit kar deta hoon, to available amount zero ho jayega aur deposited amount me add ho jayega.

## Scene 4: Daily Entry

Screen: Daily Entry tab

Action:

1. Date field dikhayein.
2. Cash Earning me `800` type karein.
3. Online Earning me `350` type karein.
4. Total Earning auto update dikhayein.
5. Expense me `200` type karein.
6. Expense Reason me `CNG` type karein.
7. Aaj ki saving auto update dikhayein.
8. `Google Sheet me Save karein` click karein.

Bolna:

Date automatic aaj ki date le leta hai. Yahan cash earning aur online earning alag-alag daalte hain. Total earning automatic calculate hoti hai. Agar koi expense hua hai, jaise CNG, repair, parking ya challan, to expense amount aur reason likh sakte hain. Saving bhi automatic calculate hoti hai. Save karte hi data Google Sheet me chala jata hai.

## Scene 5: One Entry Per Day Rule

Screen: Daily Entry tab after saving

Action:

Save ke baad form disabled/error message dikhayein.

Bolna:

Is website me ek din me sirf ek entry allowed hai. Agar same date par dobara entry karne ki koshish karenge, to error aayega ki data already entered. Isse duplicate record nahi banega.

## Scene 6: Earnings Tab

Screen: Earnings tab

Action:

1. Earnings tab click karein.
2. Summary cards dikhayein:
   - Last 7 Days Earning
   - This Month Earning
   - Total Earning
   - Total Expense
   - Net Saving
3. Records table dikhayein.

Bolna:

Earnings tab me Google Sheet se report fetch hoti hai. Yahan last 7 days earning, current month earning, total earning, total expense aur net saving dikh jati hai. Neeche full daily records bhi table me dikhte hain.

## Scene 7: Deposit Now

Screen: Money Wallet card

Action:

1. Available amount dikhayein.
2. `Deposit Now` button click karein.
3. Available amount zero hone ka result dikhayein.
4. Earnings tab me Bank Deposit History dikhayein.

Bolna:

Jab mere paas jo cash aur online amount available hai, use bank me deposit karna ho, to Deposit Now button click karunga. Click karte hi available amount bank deposit me chala jayega. Available amount zero ho jayega, aur Bank Deposit History me deposit record save ho jayega. Agle din earning add karne par available amount phir badhne lagega.

## Scene 8: Google Sheet Proof

Screen: Google Sheet

Action:

1. `Auto Earnings` sheet open karein.
2. Daily entry columns dikhayein.
3. `Bank Deposits` sheet open karein.
4. Deposit record dikhayein.

Bolna:

Yahan Google Sheet me Auto Earnings sheet daily entries ke liye hai. Isme date, cash earning, online earning, total earning, expense, reason aur balance save hota hai. Bank Deposits sheet me deposit date, cash deposit, online deposit aur total deposit save hota hai.

## Scene 9: APK Reminder

Screen: Android APK project ya app explanation

Bolna:

Is website ka Android APK source bhi ready hai. App ka naam Auto Diary hai. Isme 9:00 PM ka default reminder hai, jo notification dega ki aaj ka data entry karo. Reminder ka time app me change bhi kiya ja sakta hai.

## Scene 10: Outro

Screen: Dashboard

Bolna:

Is tarah Auto Diary website daily earning, expense, saving, deposit aur Google Sheet report ko automatic manage karti hai. Bas daily entry save karni hai, aur jab paisa bank me deposit karna ho to Deposit Now click karna hai.
